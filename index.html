Unforgettable landscapes

The Maldives is a tropical paradise, known for its crystal clear waters, white sandy beaches, and vibrant coral reefs. The landscape is dominated by palm trees and lush vegetation, with an abundance of exotic wildlife and marine life. The Maldives is a popular destination for scuba diving and snorkeling, offering a glimpse into the incredible diversity of the underwater world. The Maldives is also famous for its overwater bungalows and luxurious resorts.

Place of your dreams

Overwater bungalows in Maldives are luxurious structures built on stilts above the water. They offer stunning views of the ocean and coral reefs, and feature thatched roofs and private decks. They are perfect for swimming and snorkeling, some of them even come with infinity pools. Our bungalows are a unique way to experience the Maldives.

Offers

Private beach

The hotel has a private beach, perfect for guests looking for a secluded and exclusive experience. The beach is lined with palm trees and lush vegetation, providing a tropical and idyllic setting. Guests can enjoy a variety of activities on the beach, such as sunbathing, swimming, and snorkeling in the crystal-clear waters. The private beach is an ideal spot to relax and unwind, away from the crowds.

We provide guests with lounge chairs, umbrellas, towels, and sunscreen on the private beach, ensuring that they have everything they need to relax and enjoy the sun in comfort.

Amazing sunsets

The hotel offers a variety of special events and experiences on the private beach, such as romantic beachside dinners and ceremonies. Guests can also plan special events such as weddings, anniversary celebrations, and birthday parties on the beach. The hotel staff will work with guests to create a customized and unforgettable experience

The hotel also offer to organize private beach parties and BBQs, with live music and entertainment, providing a unique and memorable way to celebrate any occasion.

We love animals

Our resort is pet-friendly, welcoming guests to bring their furry friends along for their stay. The hotel provides designated pet-friendly rooms, and offers amenities such as pet beds, bowls, and treats. The hotel also has a designated pet area on the private beach, where guests can enjoy the sun and sand with their pets. The hotel staff are available to provide assistance and make sure that guests and their pets have a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

It's important to note that guests should inform the hotel in advance if they plan to bring a pet, in order to ensure that a pet-friendly room is available and to make any necessary arrangements.
